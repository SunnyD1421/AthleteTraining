Welcome to Dylan Sund's Baseball Training Data Manager/Scheduler

This app is my senior capstone project for a B.S. in Data Analytics at Westmont College.

Who is this for?

Initially, I wanted to be able to use this app for keeping track of my own stats for Weight Lifting and Throwing. My goal would be to have someone on the baseball team create an account and log some of their data into the database.

What are the goals of the project?

At the beginning, I didn't know what I needed to learn in order to create a webapp with Django. Throughout the creation so far, I have learned a little bit of

  1. HTML
  2. CSS
  3. JavaScript
  4. Django
  5. and better Python
    6.

The project will give the user the ability to plan workouts, enter their data, and visualize that data in tables or graphs. The visualization will be done automatically for the user. This allows for almost anyone to be able to learn from the data they have instead of just looking at it in an excel sheet or even a physical journal.

The biggest feature that I do not have implemented yet was the calendar and scheduling aspect. Before the last milestone, I added some models that will support it. This dashboard has a datepicker that will show the workouts planned on the day that is chosen.
